About Me: My Story :)

Hey! My name's Justin, and I'm a 16 year old incoming first year at UNSW studying Advanced Maths and Computer Science.

I actually wasn't always all that interested in programming: I'd always been incredibly bogged down by all the 'syntactic sugar' as I now call it. I was pretty confident that I was going to study maths (despite me facing similar issues with it in my junior years haha) and I knew I wanted a double degree, but I wasn't too sure what to choose.

I'm super stoked to be even considered for the trainee program, and am really looking forward to gaining experience in frontend development and more experience programming generally as I am still quite new - I actually skipped three weeks to the web development section of the course to develop this website but I'll be doubling back to have a crack at Python and SQL :).

Feel free to fly to the CBD and Iraq to learn more about me! Again, super excited to gain some experience in React and especially web development.
